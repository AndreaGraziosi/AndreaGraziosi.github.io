Personal Journeys

I was born into a creative dynamic environment of engineers artists and conservationists. My grandafathers were one, an engineering architect, inventor, responsible for building much of suburban Buenos Aires, married to a humanitarian granmother who started foundations to help the poor in Argentina. A Hollywood movie maker grandpa and actor grandma who contributed to the beginning of the movement to publicize conservation of our planet through their films about nature and the creation of a foundation which purchased and preserved hundreds of thousands of acres of Amazon Rain Forest. My parents, an architect dad, and an artist mother, raised us 4 kids in the heart of the Atlantic rainforest in the sate of Rio de Janeiro, Brasil. We helped our parents build our own home, and from an early age were introduced to the workshop and tools and encouraged to build tinker and think. We repaired TV antenas, created incredible toys, made blueprints for clothing and small structures, painted murals and understood the beautiful and delicate balance of the natural environment all around us. The possibilities are endless the world is a beautiful place. I am here to contribute and to never stop learning, exploring and sharing.
